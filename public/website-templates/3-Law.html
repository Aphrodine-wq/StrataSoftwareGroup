Our Mission

Committed to excellence in legal representation—and to treating every client like they’re our only one.

Henderson & Associates was founded in 1986 on the principle that everyone deserves access to skilled, compassionate legal representation. Robert Henderson opened the firm after a decade as a prosecutor, with a focus on leveling the playing field for injury victims and families. Today we combine that courtroom experience with personalized attention so you always know where your case stands.

Practice Areas

We offer full-service representation for individuals and businesses. Each practice area is led by attorneys who have tried cases in state and federal court.

Personal Injury

Auto accidents, trucking crashes, slip and fall, and medical malpractice. We recover maximum compensation for medical bills, lost wages, and pain and suffering. Example: we routinely handle commercial truck accident cases with seven-figure policy limits and catastrophic injury claims.

Business Law

Contract drafting and disputes, entity formation (LLC, corporation), partnership agreements, and breach of contract litigation. We advise small and midsize businesses on day-to-day risk and represent them in court when negotiations fail.

Family Law

Divorce, custody, child and spousal support, and adoption. We approach family matters with sensitivity while fighting for your interests—whether that’s shared custody, asset division, or enforcing a support order.

Criminal Defense

Misdemeanors to felonies: DUI, drug charges, assault, theft, and white-collar matters. We represent clients from arrest through trial and appeal. Early involvement often leads to reduced or dismissed charges; we respond 24/7 for arrests.
