Our Coverage

We offer clear policy options with no hidden fees. Choose the coverage that fits your life—from first-time renters to long-term homeowners and everyone in between.

Home Insurance

Protect your home and belongings with flexible dwelling and personal property limits. We cover replacement cost for your structure and optional scheduled items like jewelry and electronics. Optional flood and earthquake endorsements available in eligible areas.

Auto Insurance

Competitive rates with liability, collision, and comprehensive options. State minimums to high limits (e.g. 250/500/100) available. Add rental reimbursement, gap coverage, or roadside assistance to match how you drive.

Life Insurance

Term life (10–30 years) or whole life to secure your family’s future. Coverage amounts from $100,000 to $2M+; no medical exam options for qualified applicants. Convert term to permanent later without reapplying.

Health Insurance

Individual and family plans with broad provider networks. Deductibles from $0 to $10K; HSA-eligible plans available. Prescription, mental health, and preventive care included per ACA guidelines.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most. Can’t find yours? Call 1-800-555-0100 or email hello@secureguard.com.

How quickly can I get coverage? +
Most policies can be activated within 24 hours. Online applications are processed immediately during business hours (8am–8pm ET). If you apply outside those hours, we’ll process it first thing the next business day and send your documents by email.
Do you offer discounts? +
Yes. We offer multi-policy (bundle) discounts, safe driver discounts, and home security discounts (e.g. monitored alarm, impact-resistant roofing). Bundling home and auto typically saves 15–25% depending on your profile. Ask your agent for a full list of eligibility.
How do I file a claim? +
File claims 24/7 online in the customer portal or by calling our claims hotline at 1-800-555-0100 (option 2). You’ll get a claim number and an adjuster assignment. Most straightforward claims are processed within 5–7 business days; complex or high-value claims may take longer with updates at each step.
Can I manage my policy online? +
Yes. Our customer portal lets you view all policies, make payments, download ID cards, update your address or vehicles, and file claims anytime. You can also set up autopay and paperless billing to avoid missing a due date.
What if I need to cancel or change my policy? +
You can make changes or request cancellation online or by phone. If you cancel mid-term, we’ll refund any unearned premium (usually within 10 business days). There are no cancellation fees. If you’re switching vehicles or moving, update your policy as soon as possible so coverage stays accurate.
Do you cover my area? +
We’re licensed in all 50 states for auto and home insurance. Life and health products vary by state due to regulations. Enter your ZIP on the quote form or call us—we’ll confirm availability and any state-specific options (e.g. flood, earthquake) for your address.
