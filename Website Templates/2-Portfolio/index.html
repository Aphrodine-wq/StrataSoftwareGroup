About Me

I'm a multidisciplinary creative with 8+ years of experience in UX design and front-end development. I started in visual design, moved into interaction and prototyping, and now spend most of my time in the browser—building design systems and shipping features in React. I love solving complex problems with clean, user-centered solutions and believe the best work happens when design and development stay in the same room.

When I'm not designing or coding, you'll find me hiking, shooting film photography, or experimenting in the kitchen. I'm based in Portland and work with startups and product teams remotely.

Brand Redesign
