Our Story

For over a century, ÉLÉGANCE has defined the pinnacle of craftsmanship and design. Founded in Geneva in 1912, the house began as a small atelier of horologists and jewellers who refused to compromise on materials or technique. Each piece was—and still is—signed by the artisan who created it.

Today we remain family-owned and operate from our historic headquarters on the Place du Molard, with salons in Paris, London, and New York. We do not advertise. Our clients find us through referral, and our collections are presented by appointment or at invitation-only events. We believe luxury is not about visibility but about integrity: the integrity of the craft, the materials, and the relationship with those who appreciate it.

Each piece tells a story of dedication, heritage, and unparalleled quality. When you wear or live with ÉLÉGANCE, you become part of that story.

Collections

HORLOGERIE

Timepieces crafted in-house: complications, limited editions, and bespoke movements. From dress watches to tourbillons, each is tested for 21 days before it leaves the atelier.

JOAILLERIE

High jewellery and signature pieces. We work only with conflict-free stones and reclaimed gold; many designs are one-of-a-kind or made to order for our members.

MODE

Ready-to-wear and accessories that bridge tradition and modernity. Tailoring and leather goods produced in limited runs in our European ateliers.

ART DE VIVRE

Objects for the home: tableware, fragrance, and objets d’art. Designed to last generations and to be passed down, not replaced.
